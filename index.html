About The Conference

Praise the Lord! I am writing to invite you all to our annual Revival and Prayer Conference taking place here in CMFI Maryland from Wednesday, August 3rd to Sunday, August 7th, 2016 under the theme Equipping the Saints For the End Time Awakening. We are blessed to have Brother Sadhu Sundar Selvaraj as our main speaker at this conference.

The Revival Conference 2016 is a unique event not to miss. Christians are coming from all part of the world to attend this powerful event. We strongly ask that all who desire to attend this conference inform us as soon as possible. Brother Sadhu Sundar Selvaraj is known worldwide and many who have heard about this program have already made plans to attend. The attendance projected at this time leaves us with limited room capacity. Therefore, the earlier you let us know your intention to attend the easier it will be for us to secure your spaces. Lodging arrangements will be made according to the number of out-of-state participants we will have. We therefore ask those who will need accommodation to please let us know in advance so we can plan accordingly. For all our out-of-state brethren, please let us know when you are landing so we can pick you up at the airport or train stations. Attendance is free.

We entreat you to keep this program in your prayers. Prayer topics will be circulated in due time. The Lord our God will do great things. The glory of God will be manifested at these meetings and the Lord will be present to save, heal, deliver, teach, and transform. We implore you not to miss this glorious occasion. We are expecting amazing things to happen in our midst. Our prayer is that permission and visas to attend will be granted to all who need it. You will not regret any sacrifices made to partake in this monumental event.

Speaker Biography

Br. Sadhu Selvaraj

Br. Sadhu Sundar Selvaraj is a forerunner who has been privileged to be called to prepare the way for the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. He has been walking in the secret place with God for well over three decades. Brother Sadhu, as he is affectionate called, has had numerous encounters with the Lord and has been commissioned by the Lord Jesus Christ to Prepare the Bride for His second coming. For sixteen years, he pioneered an apostolic work to the unreached people of Tibet in Asia where he saw great breakthrough over the powers of darkness and lasting fruit. In 2005, at the command of the Lord, he launched Angel TV, a 24-hour Christian television network, as an end-time prophetic voice to prepare the nations for the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. Today, Angel TV reaches out to approximately half the globe, well over 125 nations. His life and ministry attested with miracles, signs, and wonders will teach, encourage, and provoke you to deepen your walk with Christ. The strong anointing and the Word of the Lord that flows through Brother Sadhu’s life stirs, encourages, and edifies the saints to live a holy life and to walk intimately with God as the Bride of Christ.

Brother Sadhu’s prophetic ministry to the nations has led him to Asia and some remote parts of the world with visions, supernatural revelations, and specific warnings of judgment. The Holy Spirit works through His servant to bring godly sorrow, true repentance, and a turning from idolatry to the one true God. Since 1979, the Lord has taken Brother Sadhu to more than 50 nations around the world to be a witness for Him. His ministry in America last year brought deep revival to our church and awakened believers to the heart of God for America. It generated a corporate movement of fasting and prayer for the nation that we intend to keep alive till the Lord’s return.

Dr Bruce Allen is the founder of Still Waters International Ministries. He is an author, as well as a well-known international conference speaker and prophetic teacher to the nations. It is Dr Bruce’s passion to see the Body of Christ awaken to their destiny in this hour by equipping them to hear God’s voice and access the reality of the supernatural in every day life. Dr Bruce and his wife, Reshma, live in the Spokane area of Washington, and work together in ministry to provoke a generation to the reality of the Kingdom of God among us and to prepare the way of the Lord.

Event FAQs

When does the church open her door each day?

The Church is open for everyone to come in at least 2hours prior to service time.

Can I have the speaker of each session?

Br Sadhu is the main speaker of the conference. He will be speaker each day of the conference.

Is your building handicap accessible?

Yes, our building is handicap accessible. Please be sure to let us know when registering, so we can reserve a set for you.

Is there any deadline to registration?

Registration is free and will remain free. However, due to the limited capacity, we may stop registration if we have reached our maximum capacity

Will you live stream all sessions of this conference?

We plan to live stream the conference. We will keep the website updated with the final decision.
